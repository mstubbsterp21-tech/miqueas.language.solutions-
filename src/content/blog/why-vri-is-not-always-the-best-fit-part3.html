A Pathway to Better Communication

To help your organization make the right choice, consider this simple planning guide:

  1. Inquiry: Ask the Deaf participant their preference. This is the most critical step in access policy.
  2. Environment Audit: Is the internet stable? Is the lighting sufficient? Is the screen large enough?
  3. Risk Assessment: Is this a high-stakes, complex, or highly interactive meeting?
  4. Credential Review: Does the interpreter have the specific skills needed for this content?

By following these standards, your organization moves beyond basic compliance and into true inclusion.
